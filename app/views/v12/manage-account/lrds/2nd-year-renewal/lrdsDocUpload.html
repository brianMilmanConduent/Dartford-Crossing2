Send your proof of eligibility

To qualify for the local resident discount, you need to send documents that prove your eligibility.

All documents must show your name and address. You can upload a copy or photo of them, or send them to us by post. Screenshots are not allowed.

You need to send:

  • your vehicle V5C log book
  • two of the following:
    • a recent bank or credit card statement
    • a recent utility bill (gas, electric or water only)
    • your most recent council tax bill
    • an Income Support or housing association rent document
    • your driving licence (photo card only)
