Help with the bulk upload template

The Dart Charge bulk upload template has columns for all the details you need to add, edit, or delete. By filling in these details in the right way, you can update up to 5,000 vehicles at one time.

Adding a vehicle

You only need to complete certain fields in the template, depending on whether your vehicle is registered in the UK or with the Driver & Vehicle Licencing Agency (DVLA).

Adding a UK vehicle that’s registered with the DVLA

  • Complete columns A and E
  • If you have groups of vehicles, you can include a group name in column G. This name must be 10 characters or less

Adding a UK vehicle that’s not registered with the DVLA

  • Complete all columns A to F
  • If you have groups of vehicles, you can include a group name in column G. This name must be 10 characters or less

Adding a non-UK vehicle

  • Complete all columns A to F
  • If you have groups of vehicles, you can include a group name in column G. This name must be 10 characters or less
