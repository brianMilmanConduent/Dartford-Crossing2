How do you want to send your proof of local residency?

To qualify for the local resident discount, you need to send documents that prove your residency and vehicle ownership details. You can upload a copy of the documents, or send them to us by post.

Required documents

All documents must show your name and address. You need to send a copy of:

  • A current council tax bill
  • Your vehicle V5C log book
  • One of the following:
    • a bank or credit card statement no more than 3 months old
    • a utility bill (gas, electric or water only) no more than 3 months old
    • an Income Support or housing association rent document
    • your driving licence
